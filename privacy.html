Privacy Policy

Your data never leaves your device

Last Updated: May 4, 2026
🔒 Our Privacy Promise: Pixel Craft Tool processes all images 100% locally in your browser. No uploads. No servers. No tracking. Your photos never leave your device.

1. What We Don't Collect

Unlike most online tools, Pixel Craft Tool does NOT:

  • ❌ Upload your images to any server
  • ❌ Store your photos or editing history
  • ❌ Track your IP address or location
  • ❌ Use cookies for advertising or analytics (except essential PWA cookies)
  • ❌ Share your data with third parties

2. How Pixel Craft Tool Works

All image processing happens directly in your browser using modern web technologies (Canvas API, WebAssembly). When you edit a photo:

  1. You select a file from your device
  2. The file is loaded into your browser's memory
  3. All edits are applied locally
  4. The result is downloaded back to your device
  5. Your browser automatically clears the temporary data

No data is ever transmitted to our servers because we don't have servers for image processing.

3. What Data We Might See

The only information we might receive is:

  • 📊 Anonymous usage statistics via Google Analytics (if enabled) — no personal data
  • 🐛 Error reports if you choose to submit a bug report (optional)
  • 💬 Messages if you contact us via email

You can disable analytics in your browser settings or by using privacy-focused extensions.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Pixel Craft Tool uses minimal browser storage for:

  • 🎨 Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • ⚙️ Tool settings (last used size, format, etc.)
  • 🔐 PWA installation status

All data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared anytime via browser settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Pixel Craft Tool is designed to be safe for all ages. Since we don't collect personal data, there are no special restrictions for users under 13 (COPPA compliant by design).

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Pixel Craft Tool constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
